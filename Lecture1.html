Lecture 1: 你好，你叫什么名字？

You probably know what that first phrase is, but the rest is saying "what is your name?". If you were to respond to that, it would be "我叫Jacob."
But what is 我? What is 你? Well, those characters are identified as nouns. I'll give you a list of them right now!

Nouns (名词)

Character Pinyin Meaning
我 wǒ I, my
你 nǐ you, your
他/她 tā he/she, his/her
我们 wǒ men we, our
他们* tā men they, them
*她们 also works, but only when the "they" includes all females, and all females only. It's not necessary to use it in that situation though~

Let's give you a few examples:

  • 我叫婷婷。 (My name is 婷婷.)
  • 他叫云云。 (His name is 云云.)
  • 我的妈妈叫立岩。 (My mother's name is 立岩.)
  • 你的妈妈叫Heidi。 (Your mother's name is Heidi.)

Exercise 1: Make a list of friends and/or family, and type out what their names are in complete sentences. Make sure to include a group of friends/family
and yourself at least. Since it's your first exercise I'll give you a small hint that may come in handy: the word "and" would be 和.

Tone Marks (音标)

You've already seen tone marks from the table under the Pinyin column, but what do they mean?

Tone marks are used to pronunciate Chinese characters. What makes tone marks convenient is the fact that there are only 4 tones. Of course this website

CNY Project: Lion and Dragon Dance

This will be the start of a long project to celebrate Chinese New Year! This special holiday starts on February 1st, 2022, and ends on February 11th.
Although the dates are far away, there's still time to prepare and learn more about its festivities! Two of the most common festivities are the Dragon Dance
and the Lion Dance.

During the Lion Dance, two performers would be play the role as the lion; the person in the front is the head of the lion, and the person in the back is the
rear of the lion. They would mimic the lion's movements in order to bring good luck and fortune. Here's a video of a team performing at the World Lion Dance
Championship. You'll be able to see the swift movements that make the lion fully animated!

The Dragon Dance involves multiple performers. They would have to use poles to handle the movement of the dragon as it tries to look for the pearl in the front.
The longer the dragon is in the dance, the more fortune and good luck you're provided. The dance starts at 1:41.
